About Me

My name is Chen Fanggege, but I prefer being called as Gege since 'Fang' is from my mother's name. Gege in Chinese is a way to address a princess in ancient time, that is part of reason why my parents gave me this name - I'm my parents' princess. It is a bit difficult and embarrassing to explain my name to people whom I first meet, but it is so COOL to have such a honourable name.

I came to Singapore in January 2021. Now I'm an international student in Singapore Polytechnic, studying in diploma of Infocomm Security Management.

My Travel Experience

Since primary 3, I started travelling around the world (technically not the world, Asia only) I've been to many countries: Japan, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Russia, Vietnam... These wonderful experiences really broaden my horizons and make me understand the other cultures more. Every country has its own feature, scenary and food. These experiences make me different from my peers, influencing my insight and worldview.

The first time I came to Singapore was also for travelling, right after my primary school graduation(2017) I visited Marina Bay, Clarke Quay, Sentosa Island... Every place a person should visit in Singapore. Walking on the path in NUS, I've never thought that one day I'll be staying here and pursuing my academics.
